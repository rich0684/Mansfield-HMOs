The Opportunity

Seven years of management data. No projections required.

1 Clifton Place is one of two HMOs being offered for sale by their current owner. Purseglove Property has managed this property for over seven years — an unusually long track record that gives us a level of operational transparency most deals simply do not have.

The figures in this document are not projections. They are drawn directly from two full years of profit and loss accounts. You are seeing what this property has actually earned and what it has actually cost to run.

We know this property intimately — the tenants, the maintenance history, the cost base, the income profile. Most acquisitions involve a degree of trust in numbers you cannot independently verify. Here, the managing agent is the one presenting the data.

Purseglove will continue to manage the property following any change of ownership for investors who want a fully hands-off position.
